What is Commander Ranked?

Commander Ranked is a community-driven system for evaluating and comparing Commander decks. Each card legal in Commander is assigned a point value through user-submitted rankings. When you submit a deck on the Rank Your Own Deck page, its total score is calculated by summing the points of all included cards — giving you a consistent, transparent way to compare your deck’s power level with your playgroup or opponents.

How It Works

On the Rank These Cards page, you're presented with four random Commander-legal cards. Your task is to order them from strongest to weakest based on your personal evaluation. Each placement assigns the cards a point value — higher rankings earn more points, while lower rankings lose points. These point totals are aggregated and tracked on the Leaderboard tab, where you can explore how individual cards are performing overall. Whether you're searching for your favorite pet card or exploring new additions for your deck, the leaderboard offers insight into how the community values each card.
